About The Summit

Preparing Youths for a Sustainable Future

The Sustainability Youth Summit 2025 is a platform for young people to engage with experts, policymakers, and industry leaders to explore opportunities and solutions for a sustainable future.

Organized by Pioneer Child Development Programme and Karatina University, this summit aims to equip youths with knowledge and strategies to promote sustainable practices in their communities.

Pioneer CDP
Co-organizer
Karatina University
Host Institution

Our Mission

To inspire and equip young people with the knowledge, skills, and networks they need to become sustainability champions in their communities.

Key Focus Areas

Environmental Sustainability

Equipping youths with knowledge and strategies to promote sustainable practices.

Green Careers

Exploring opportunities in the green economy and future job market.

Innovation

Showcasing innovations that contribute to climate resilience.

Key Activities

Showcase Innovation

Exhibitions

Showcase innovative projects and solutions for sustainability challenges. Each institution will have representation for a maximum of 10 students and can exhibit innovative projects.

Connect with Experts

Mentorship & Networking

Connect with industry experts, leaders, and like-minded individuals passionate about sustainability. Build relationships that can accelerate your sustainability journey.

Engage in Dialogue

Panel Discussions

Engage in thought-provoking discussions on key sustainability themes, led by experts and policymakers. Share your perspectives and learn from diverse viewpoints.

9:30 AM - 11:00 AM

Keynote Presentations
